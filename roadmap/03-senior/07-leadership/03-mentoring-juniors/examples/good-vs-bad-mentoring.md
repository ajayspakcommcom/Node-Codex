# Good Vs Bad Mentoring

## Bad

- giving the answer immediately without explaining tradeoffs
- rewriting the junior engineer’s work without teaching
- reviewing only for correctness and ignoring learning gaps
- delegating critical production work without support or guardrails
- making the junior dependent on constant approval

## Good

- explaining why a design is safer or more maintainable
- asking questions that help the engineer reason through the issue
- giving targeted feedback tied to behavior and system impact
- calibrating task scope to match growth and risk
- helping the engineer build confidence and judgment over time
