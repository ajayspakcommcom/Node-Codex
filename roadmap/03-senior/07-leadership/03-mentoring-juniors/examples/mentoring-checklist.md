# Mentoring Checklist

- Does the junior engineer understand the problem context, not just the task?
- Have tradeoffs and production implications been explained clearly?
- Is the support helping them reason independently instead of only copying a solution?
- Are expectations around testing, observability, and rollback behavior clear?
- Is feedback specific enough to improve future decisions?
- Is the task scoped appropriately for growth without unsafe risk?
- Is there a clear path to ask for help before they get blocked too long?
