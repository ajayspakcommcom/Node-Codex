# Platform Engineering

## Purpose

This section is about building the internal platforms, standards, and governance models that let many teams deliver services consistently at scale.

## Enterprise-Level Pointers

- internal developer platforms as productized internal systems
- paved-road engineering vs uncontrolled service sprawl
- API governance and contract stewardship at organization scale
- service mesh adoption boundaries and operational tradeoffs
- cost optimization as an engineering and platform concern
- platform ownership models and service-team interfaces
- guardrails vs bottlenecks in platform design
- self-service workflows with policy enforcement
- golden paths, templates, and upgrade strategy
- multi-team rollout and compatibility management
- common production mistakes
- maintainability rules
- interview-style questions
- practice exercises

## What Expert Teams Optimize For

- faster service delivery without sacrificing standards
- safer defaults across security, observability, and operations
- fewer one-off platform decisions in application teams
- platform abstractions that reduce toil instead of hiding critical behavior

## Common Production Mistakes

- building a platform with no clear product ownership
- forcing every team into the same abstraction regardless of fit
- treating governance as documentation instead of enforcement
- adopting mesh or cost controls without operational readiness
- shipping platform tooling that is harder to use than manual workflows

## Maintainability Rules

- treat platform capabilities as products with owners, adoption plans, and support boundaries
- make paved roads visible, well-documented, and easier than custom paths
- encode governance in reviewable policy and automation where possible
- measure developer experience and operational outcomes, not just platform feature count

## Interview Questions

- What makes a good internal platform instead of just shared infrastructure?
- How do you introduce governance without blocking delivery teams?
- When is a service mesh worth its operational cost?
- Why should FinOps be part of platform engineering instead of only finance?

## Practice Exercises

- Define a paved-road platform for launching a new backend service in your organization.
- Create an API governance model with contract review and versioning rules.
- Design a service mesh adoption plan with clear boundaries and rollback criteria.
- Build a cost-optimization checklist for compute, storage, and data transfer usage.
